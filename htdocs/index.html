About us

Who are we?

We are a friendly community where everybody is welcome. We are the 1st Airborne clan, founded by a small group of friends that originated from the game Hell Let Loose. The 1stA clan is an inclusive gaming community, our top priority is having fun and we treat each other with respect. You are required to have a microphone to play with us. We strive to become a bigger clan with our own server in the future. We are recruiting. The 1stA clan is always looking for new fun people to join our community of players (for competition or for fun).

What do we offer?

  • We are going to be active in SLB (squad line battles) and want to set up competition matches for Hell let Loose.
  • In the future we want to participate in league matches.

What games do we play?

Our main game is Hell let loose, a realistic squad shooter based on a WWII game.
